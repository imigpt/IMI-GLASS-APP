# Privacy Policy for IMI Glass App

**Last Updated: February 23, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the IMI Glass App ("Application," "App," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use our Application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By installing and using the IMI Glass App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use this Application.

## 2. What Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Voice Commands & Audio**: When you use voice interaction features (e.g., "Hey Imi"), we collect and process your audio input for speech recognition and AI response generation.
- **Account Information**: Email address, user profile information, and authentication credentials (if you create an account).
- **Contact Information**: If you grant permission, we access your device contacts to enable features like smart calling and contact-based queries.
- **Calendar & Events**: Calendar data may be collected with your permission for scheduling features.
- **Photos & Videos**: Media files from your device storage when you access gallery features or upload content.
- **Location Data**: GPS and coarse location information when you permit location access for features like directions and local services.
- **Feedback & Support**: Information you provide when contacting our support team, including conversations and attachments.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and hardware specifications.
- **App Usage Data**: Features accessed, timestamps of interactions, session duration, and crash reports.
- **Bluetooth Connections**: Data about paired Bluetooth devices and connection status.
- **Network Information**: Wi-Fi network names, signal strength, and network connection type.
- **Performance Data**: App performance metrics, processing times, and error logs.
- **Camera & Visual Data**: When using camera features, visual input is processed locally and/or sent to AI services based on your configuration.

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

- **Google Services**: Information integrated through Google APIs and services.
- **Spotify Data**: When using music playback features, minimal data is shared with Spotify (song choices, playback status).
- **YouTube Data**: Search queries and video preferences when using YouTube integration.
- **AI Services**: Conversation data sent to Google Gemini API and/or other AI providers for processing user queries.

## 4. Data Processing & Sensitive Information

### 4.1 AI Processing

The Application processes user queries and audio through Google's Gemini API and potentially other AI services. This means:

- **Audio files** may be transmitted to AI servers for speech-to-text and response generation
- **Visual data** (camera input) may be sent to vision AI for analysis when you use image recognition features
- **Text queries** are sent to AI servers for processing
- Processing occurs in real-time and data is typically not permanently stored by third-party AI providers (subject to their privacy policies)

**Important**: Review the privacy policies of Google Gemini, OpenAI, and other third-party services we integrate with, as they have their own data handling practices.

### 4.2 Sensitive Personal Information

The Application may access sensitive data including:
- **Biometric Data**: Voice recognition patterns
- **Location**: Precise GPS coordinates
- **Contacts & Communications**: Phone numbers, names, email addresses
- **Health & Fitness**: Steps, activity data (if device sync enabled)
- **Financial Data**: If integration with payment services is enabled

All sensitive data is handled with appropriate encryption and access controls.

## 5. Data Storage & Retention

### 5.1 Local Storage
- Conversation history and memory is stored locally on your device by default
- Contact cache and media references are stored locally
- Local data persists until you manually clear it or uninstall the app

### 5.2 Cloud Storage
- Some features may store data on secure cloud servers (with your consent)
- Backup data is encrypted during transmission and storage
- You can disable cloud sync features in app settings

### 5.3 Data Retention Periods
- **Voice recordings**: Temporarily retained for processing, then deleted (unless saved)
- **Conversation history**: Retained locally until deletion; cloud copies may be retained per backup settings
- **Usage logs**: Retained for up to 90 days for diagnostics
- **Account data**: Retained for as long as your account exists plus 30 days after deletion

## 7. Your Privacy Rights & Controls

### 7.1 Permissions Management

You can control what data the app accesses through Android permissions:
- **Camera**: Grant or deny camera access
- **Microphone**: Control audio recording permissions
- **Location**: Enable precise or approximate location only
- **Contacts**: Allow or deny contact list access
- **Media**: Permit or restrict access to photos, videos, and audio files
- **Calendar**: Grant or deny calendar access

You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

### 7.2 App-Level Controls

Within the Application:
- **Clear Conversation History**: Delete all stored conversations
- **Disable Location Tracking**: Turn off location services within the app
- **Disable Cloud Backup**: Opt out of cloud data storage
- **Adjust Privacy Settings**: Configure data collection preferences
- **Device-Only Mode**: Process data locally without sending to cloud services (where available)

### 7.3 Data Access & Deletion

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Withdraw consent for specific data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address provided in Section 10.
